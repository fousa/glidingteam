The Belgian Gliding Team wishes you a warm welcome on its website.We soon hope to see you at one of the nice airports that Belgium has, or preferably somewhere in the air.

This becomes clear if we take a look at the achievements of our national team, both juniors, as the elite. In addition to participating in regional competitions and Belgian Championships, our pilots deliver strong performances in international competitions all over the world.

Already in the 70’s famous pilots as Bert Zegels, Henri Stouffs and Paul Bourgard in the men’s team and GeoGeo Litt in the women’s team put the Belgian gliding scene on the world map. Amongst the other good results, our country delivered a vice-world champion and a vice-european champion. Today, the Belgian selection has grown considerably in depth and we are proud that both the junior and the elite teams have a very high potential.
