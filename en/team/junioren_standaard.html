Officially I took up gliding in 2009 at the age of 16 years, but in reality I have been involved in gliding, since I was born, due to the fact that I was born at an airport, Keiheuvel. After waiting for 16 years to be allowed to fly solo, this turned out to be a nice memory. That very same year I received my cross country licence and acchieved a 5th place at the Keiheuvelcup 2009.

In 2010, I have not flown that much because of crewing for my cousin, Dennis Huybreckx during the World CHampionships in Slovakia. Exactly in that period of the year, Belgium got some "booming weather". But then, I learned a lot in Slovakia! This year I also accomplished my first 500km flight, in a team with Jef Kell, a flight of 608km to Oerlinghausen and back!

My favorite flight so far is the one I made ​​after the World Championship^s in Prievidza. Unfortunately, I had a low point at Weelde, otherwise it could even have been bigger!
