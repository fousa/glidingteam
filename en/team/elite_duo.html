Name Tijl Schmelzer (Pilot)

Experience 1.800 h
Cross country 90.000 km
Glider Duo Discus XLT
Best results 8th place EGC elite / Pociunai (LIT)
  1st place International Klippeneck-Competition 2006 (DE)
  1st place Cup of Flanders 2009 (BE)

Since my grandfather built his first sailplane when he was 14 years old, gliding has been our family’s obsession. I started learning the ropes when I was 13 years old from my grandfather and father. From then on, I tried to fly as much as possible.

Since then I had the most incredible experiences in our beautiful sport. I flew almost 2000 hours and many kilometers over our flat country, as well as in the great Alps, over the grain fields of Australia and over the deserts of Namibia. My true passion is competition flying, and previously I competed in 6 World championships. My greatest triumph up till now was an 8th spot in the overall standard class ranking in the European championships at Pociunai 2007, while my brother and teammate ended up in the 6th place.

The first large international competition I participated in was the Junior Worlds in 2003 at Nitra, where I was at that time the youngest pilot.  Going back to this great place to compete in the Europeans is for me a great occasion to see which progress I have made in the past 8 years.  I am especially excited to fly together with my father in a doubleseater, as we have done last year successfully in the Worlds as well.
