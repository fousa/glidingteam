To determine the hierarchy between the Belgian pilots, all submitted contest results of each pilot are used to calculate the national rankings. Major competitions, such as Belgian, European and World Championships, give you more points than the smaller, mostly regional competitions. The exact scoring and rules are established by the Belgian Gliding Federation.
