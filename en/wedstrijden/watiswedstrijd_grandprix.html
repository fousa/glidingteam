  • Grand - Prix

To simplify the complex scoring system at traditional gliding competitions , the FAI developed early in the new millennium a new, extremely spectacular discipline, the GRAND PRIX.

The rules are very transparent, the tasks are shorter, the speeds are higher, the media attention is much bigger, the scoring is easier, ... The basic principle is that the best pilots in the world compete in the air against each other and depart all at the same time on the starting line. The rules are making it particularly easy to understand and to follow the race: The first one to cross the finish line is the winner!

Also Belgian pilots have already been present at grand prix in Germany and France. They participated in with sponsor stickers adorned gliders. Particularly spectacular are the live images, shot from the accompanying helicopters, while the cockpit cameras record every decision of the pilots meticulously. With the built-in GPS systems it is a breeze to follow the gliders at home on the computer or television monitor, accompanied by expert commentators.

Finally, the sport of glider what it is entitled to: A rising global interest and media attention which makes the sport very attractive for sponsors.
