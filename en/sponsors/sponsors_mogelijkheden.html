Gliding has in its current form, especially in competitions, a very positive development. It is both an exciting and completely environment-friendly sport, practiced by pilots who are acting very professionally. It is therefore not surprising that there are some good opportunities for sponsors.

Worldwide media attention is rising and thus at the same time the "exposure" for sponsors. From the Belgian team, there are several possibilities to focus this media attention on the sponsors. Because of the frequent participation of our pilots to large and small events, the gliders are transported across Europe in the famous long trailers. These are fully considerable as huge advertising panels which are highly visble. One can hardly wish for a better eye catcher.

Because our pilots often are in top ranked positions in various competitions, reports and interviews in all media are the order of the day. This offers of course good opportunities for sponsors to bring their product in the spotlights.
Finally, through the working group "public relations" the team has good relationships with all media. From our own contribution extremely fast reports are possible about innovations and achievements.

If your company is interested to support the Belgian Gliding Team, you can contact us.
